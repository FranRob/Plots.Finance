VLND TOKEN

Our ERC20 token, $VLND, allows game players to
own and manage virtual lands with as little as 10%
ownership. When you invest in Plots.finance, you
mint $VLND tokens that represent your
ownerships of co-owned lands. The funds raised
from investors are then used to purchase virtual
lands from select collections curated by $PLOTS
token holders, including NFT Worlds, The
Sandbox Lands, Lands by Pixels, and Otherdeeds
by Otherside.

Revenue Sharing
and Ownership

As gamers improce the virtual lands and develop games within the
co-owned metaverse, Plots.finance share revenue with $VLND and
$PLOTS holders, like financing a Roblox game developer and earning
a percentage of their in-game earnings. Gamers must improve the
lands or build game experiences within 18 months, Or Plots.finance
can sell the land and return the invested funds to the investor smart
contract for other deals.
